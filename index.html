Dr. James Holley – Lecturer in Computer Science

Qualifications:

Dr. James Holley holds a Ph.D. in Software Engineering and a Master’s degree in Computer Science. He also possesses certifications in cybersecurity and cloud computing, reflecting his expertise in modern computing systems.

Research Interests:

His research focuses on machine learning, secure software development, and cloud computing. He explores ways to enhance software security and optimize AI-driven solutions for real-world applications.

Teaching & Academic Experience:

With over 15 years of experience, Dr. Holley has taught various courses in programming, artificial intelligence, and software engineering. He has supervised graduate students and contributed to curriculum development in computer science programs. Dr. Holley also offers a variety of online courses through this platform, providing students with access to expert-led lessons in programming, machine learning, and more.
